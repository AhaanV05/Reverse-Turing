Rules & Scoring
You will be paired with either a human or an AI. Your goal is to guess who you're talking to.

Limits

  • •4 messages per person
  • •30 words max per message
  • •60 seconds to reply (session ends if you miss)

Guessing

  • •You can guess after at least 2 messages are sent in the chat (one each).
  • •When the first person guesses, the chat locks and the other person has 10 seconds to guess.
  • •If the other person does not guess in time, they get 0 points.

Scoring

  • •Wrong guess = 0 points.
  • •Correct guess = Base Score × multipliers.
  • •Base Score: 100 (minimum 5 on correct).
  • •Perfect score (best possible): 120 points.
  • •There are hidden factors that reduce your score. If you figure them out, good for you.

Bounty Bonus

  • •If you’re correct and the other person is wrong, you get a +25% bonus (capped so the final score never exceeds 120).
